Physics Wordle

This is a game similar to the popular game Wordle, but with physics equations.

Credits

Much of this game is copied from Thypher down to the code. I just wanted to have a version that can be played infinitely instead of just once a day. Please play the original game!

The word list comes from here. The equations are original, though they come from various sources, like the original Thypher, and many textbooks.
